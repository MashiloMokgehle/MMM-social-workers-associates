About Us

MMM Social Workers & Associates is a multidisciplinary team of Professional Social Workers, Occupational Therapists, Psychologists, and Associate Practitioners committed to delivering holistic and evidence-based support services. We treat people, families, organizations, and communities with discretion, morality, and compassion. By working together, we assist clients overcome life's obstacles with dignity and confidence while promoting emotional wellbeing, mental health, personal growth, healing, resilience, and long-lasting positive change.

Our Services

  • Child, Family & Disability Counselling
  • Substance Abuse & Addiction Counselling (including gambling)
  • Mediation & Divorce Support
  • Employee Wellness Support
  • Bereavement Counselling
  • Trauma Counselling
